Pasta recipe

Description

This creamy garlic Alfredo pasta is the perfect comfort food, made with tender fettuccine noodles tossed in a rich, homemade Alfredo sauce. Infused with fresh garlic, Parmesan cheese, and a touch of butter, it’s a simple yet delicious dish that’s ready in under 30 minutes. Serve it with grilled chicken or steamed vegetables for a hearty meal!

Ingredients

  • 250g fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of salted water and cook the fettuccine pasta until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the heavy cream and stir. Let it simmer for 2–3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the grated Parmesan cheese and stir until the sauce is smooth and creamy.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with chopped parsley if desired.

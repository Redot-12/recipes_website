Hamburger Recipe

Description

Juicy, flavorful, and made to satisfy, this classic hamburger features a perfectly grilled beef patty nestled in a toasted bun, topped with melted cheese, crisp lettuce, ripe tomato slices, onions, and a touch of your favorite sauce. It’s the ultimate homemade burger experience—simple, delicious, and ready to impress at any meal.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese (optional)
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 small red onion, sliced
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Ketchup, mustard, or mayonnaise
  • Butter or oil for cooking

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the ground beef with salt and pepper. Divide into 4 equal portions and shape into patties.
  2. Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat and lightly oil it.
  3. Cook the patties for about 3–4 minutes on each side, or until they reach your desired doneness.
  4. Add a slice of cheddar cheese on top of each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt (optional).
  5. Lightly toast the burger buns on the grill or in a pan.
  6. Assemble the burgers: place lettuce on the bottom bun, then the patty, tomato slices, onions, and your favorite sauces.
  7. Top with the other half of the bun and serve hot.
